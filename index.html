Frequently asked question

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number by email. You can use this tracking number to track the progress of your shipment online.

What is delivery time of my order?

Delivery times depend on your location and the shipping method selected. Typically, orders are delivered within 3-7 business days. You will receive an estimated delivery date during checkout.

How can I return my product?

You can return your product within 30 days of receipt for a full refund. Please ensure the item is unused and in its original packaging. Contact our customer service team for return instructions.

What are the shipping costs my order?

Shipping costs vary depending on the size of your order and the destination. Shipping fees will be calculated and displayed at checkout before you confirm your purchase.

Can I chance or cancel my order after pacing it?

Once an order has been placed, you can request changes or cancellations within the first 24 hours by contacting our support team. After this period, we may not be able to make changes if your order has already been processed.
